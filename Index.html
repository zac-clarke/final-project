     After college Zac went to work, mostly kitchen work which left Zac wanting more, so they decided to study carpentry. At carpentry school zac learned that carpentry was much more subjective than they had previously thought, although again creating something out of raw materials brought Zac joy.

Dirty Pizza a small restaurant in the heart of the plateau, where Zac was able to do something else they love; interacting with strangers and trying to bring a little light into their days through good food and conversation. After the Covid 19 pandemic Zac was tired of trying to keep their business afloat and decided finaly to sell their business and learn a new trade!
